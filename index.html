Wacky Fill-Ins

The Basic Idea

Basically, you have an incomplete story with blanks, and you can fill in those blanks to create a finished story that might not make much sense narratively but is unexpectedly hillarious. See README.md for a more detailed explanation of the basic idea.

How to Write Your Own Wacky Fill-Ins

You don't have to know JavaScript to write your own Wacky Fill-Ins, but it really helps to know HTML, and, to a lesser extent, CSS. You can use any of the three sample files (sample1.html, sample2.html, sample3.html) as a template.
